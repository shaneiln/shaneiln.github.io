About

Architect.
Photographer.

I'm Shaneil Narsey, an architect based in Queenstown, New Zealand. My work sits at the intersection of built form and natural landscape — the way light moves through a space, how materials age, where architecture ends and environment begins.

Photography is how I see. It sharpens the way I approach design — training attention on shadow, texture, proportion, and the quiet details that make a place feel alive.

Based in the South Island, I'm endlessly drawn to the scale and stillness of the Southern Alps, the quality of light over Lake Wanaka, and the raw materiality of the high country.
